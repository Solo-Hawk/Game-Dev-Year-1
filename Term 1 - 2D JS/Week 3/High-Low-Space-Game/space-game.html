Help:

During the story of the game there will be points where you are required to perform an action. Whether the action succeeds or not is dependent on how well you do in a game of High/Low.

In High/Low you are presented with a card with a number value between 1 and 11. You must guess whether the next card drawn will be Higher or Lower than the previous one.

Initially you have to guess 3/5 of the cards correctly to succeed, but this can change as the story progresses.

Good Luck!


Status Effects:

Burn - Currently not implemented.

Oxygen - If in low oxygen, the card to guess will receive a -1 if you guess High, or +1 if you guess Low.

Morale - Low morale will give you -1 to the total number of guesses. High morale will give you -1 to the number of correct guesses to pass.
